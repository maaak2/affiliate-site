# Affiliate Review Website — Project Requirements

## 1. Overview
A personal affiliate marketing website where the owner posts honest reviews of products, services, and hotels he has personally used, with affiliate links so visitors can purchase through his referral.

## 2. Target Market
- Primary: Saudi Arabia
- Secondary: MENA region (Arabic-speaking countries)

## 3. Languages
- Full bilingual site: Arabic and English
- Arabic must render right-to-left (RTL); English left-to-right (LTR)
- Language switcher on every page

## 4. Content Structure
Items are organized into categories (e.g., Electronics, Hotels, Services, Home Goods, etc. — categories to be defined/expandable by the owner).

Each item (product/service/hotel) needs its own page with:
- Category tag
- One or more photos and/or videos
- Owner's written review (short paragraph)
- Rating out of 10
- Price the owner actually paid
- Affiliate link(s) to buy/book — must support **two separate links** for the same item (e.g., same product listed on two different websites/retailers)
- Optional discount/promo code, shown if available

## 5. Admin / Content Management
Owner needs an easy way (CMS or admin panel) to add, edit, and organize items and categories without touching code, and to manage bilingual content (Arabic + English) for each entry.

## 6. SEO & Marketing
- Full on-page SEO: meta titles/descriptions, clean URLs, structured data (schema.org Product/Review markup), sitemap, fast page load
- Bilingual SEO (separate optimization for Arabic and English keywords)
- Support for AI-driven search discovery (so the site surfaces well when people or AI assistants search for a product/keyword)
- Analytics and tracking for affiliate click performance

## 7. Other Notes
- Mobile-friendly/responsive design
- Site should scale as more categories and items are added over time
